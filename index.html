The SMG Werks 911s are revisioned through our perspective, integrating today's advanced engineering while maintaining the timeless allure of their classic design.

At SMG Werks, we preserve the authenticity of each vehicle by maintaining its original design and structure rather than performing backdating/forward-dating modifications. Our approach focuses on enhancing the car with modern engineering and technology, ensuring improved performance and functionality.
